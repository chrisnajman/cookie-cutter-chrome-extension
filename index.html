Overview

Cookie Cutter is a simple and efficient Chrome extension designed to clear cookies for the current tab. With one click, it removes all first-party cookies for the active site. You can then go on to refresh your session.

Please note, Cookie Cutter does not delete third-party cookies, making it suitable for users who want control over cookies without disrupting other browsing data.

Screenshots

Features

  • Clear cookies for the current tab with a single click.
  • Removes only first-party cookies, leaving third-party cookies unaffected.
  • Simple interface with an easy-to-use "Clear" and optional "Reload tab?" button.
  • Lightweight and efficient—ideal for quickly managing cookies without clearing local storage or session storage.

Reviewing and Rating

To post reviews and/or rate Cookie Cutter, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Cookie Cutter Chrome Web Store Listing Page.
  2. Scroll down to the "Reviews" section.
  3. Click the "Write a review" button.
  4. If not signed in, you will be prompted to log in to your Google account.
  5. You can then rate the extension using a star rating and leave a comment.
  6. Once finished, you can click "Post" to submit the review.

Note: You need to be signed in to your Google account to post reviews or rate extensions.
